Th' colors 'o th' devices in th' image may vary, but note th' button placement 'n features 'o each device carefully to ensure ye be selectin' th' correct one.
{: .notice}

Click th' image 'o ye device to take ye to th' appropriate page.
{: .notice--primary}

Different device models, versions, 'n regions gunna require different steps to achieve th' end goal 'o arm9loaderhax Custom Firmware. This page will help you find where to start for your device.
{: .notice--info}

If ye have already hacked ye 3DS before 'n be havin' an EmuNAND based CFW setup, jus' follow all instructions while on SysNAND. Ye data gunna be transfered from EmuNAND to ye new A9LH install at th' end.
{: .notice--info}

**You will need ~1.79GB of free space on your SD card if you are using an Old 3DS or 2DS, or ~2.10GB of free space on your SD card if you are using a New 3DS.**
{: .notice--info}

Before startin', ye may want to check ye SD card fer errors usin' [H2testw (Windows)](h2testw-(windows)), [F3 (Linux)](f3-(linux)), or [F3X (Mac)](f3x-(mac))!
{: .notice--warning}
